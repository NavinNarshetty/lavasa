Recognition encourages young athletes to strive for better. For the achievers, it is a boost in confidence and for the rest, it is a benchmark to aim for. Sports For All presents SFA Awards in various categories to recognise young athletes displaying exemplary talent, skill and potential and to motivate and felicitate coaches for their hard work and dedication towards sport.

All the SFA Awards categories will be judged basis performance at SFA Mumbai {{year16}}. We are accepting nominations for SFA MasterCoach Team Award and SFA BornToLead Award from schools at: info@sfanow.in.
