Register for SFA Mumbai 2017

Every School and Athlete (Participating in team sport or individual sport) must first register themselves individually. And thereafter proceed to register themselves for their sport/sports of interest.

Step 1 : School / Athlete Registration & Verification

School Registration

Once a school registers with SFA, any number of students of the registered school will be eligible to participate in as many sports as they choose to (basis eligibility criteria).

Register your school for SFA Mumbai 2017 in just 4 simple steps:

  • Fill out the online ‘School Registration Form’ & submit for verification
  • Make a payment of Rs 12,000 (inclusive of all applicable taxes) towards registration fees
  • Receive SFA school verification along with a unique SFA ID & Password
  • Proceed to register athletes from your school for Sports Registration

Click here to view registered school benefits for SFA Mumbai 2017

Athlete Registration

Once an athlete registers with SFA, he/she can participate in as many sports as he/she chooses to (basis eligibility criteria).

Register yourself to compete at SFA Mumbai 2017 in just 4 simple steps:

  • Fill out the online ‘Athlete Registration Form’ and submit it along with a Photo ID for verification
  • Make payment of 200 (inclusive of all applicable taxes) towards registration fees
  • Receive SFA athlete verification along with a unique SFA ID & Password
  • Proceed to register for Sports Registration

Click here to view registered schools athlete benefits for SFA Mumbai 2017

Step2 : Sport Registration

Sport Registration

Once you have submitted your school / athlete registration form you will receive a verification email (within 2 working days from submission) with an SFA ID & Password

Once you are verified by SFA, you can fill and submit the forms of all the sports you wish to participate and compete in at SFA Mumbai 2017.
