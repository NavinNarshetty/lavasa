Kindly Note: This is a one-time registration process. Completion of this process will allow you to register using your SFA ID & Code for all future SFA Events. This also enables us to create a personalized sporting experience for your school on our website www.sfanow.in to highlight your performances in sport by way of profiles, records maintenance, match videos, statistics and reports.

We request you to keep the following details ready to ensure a smooth registration process:
  • Mobile Number which you wish to register with SFA
  • Email ID which you wish to register with SFA
  • Scanned copy of your Aadhar ID Card OR Passport OR Pan Card OR Birth Certificate
  • Scanned copy of your School ID Card OR School Report Card
  • Credit Card/Debit Card/Bank Details (Only in case of online payment)
  • Your profile picture in Jpeg/PNG format
*The applicant represents that the information provided is true and correct. SFA will keep confidential and protect your personal information except where disclosure is required or permitted by law.
