Squash

Indian Squash Professionals (ISP), a non-profit making organization, was formed by Mr. Mahendra Agarwal in 1993, with the sole aim of promoting the game of Squash in India. Ever since its inception, ISP has spread the game of squash to the interiors of Maharashtra through coaching camps, tournaments and by building squash courts. The ultimate goal of ISP is to identify talent and foster competitive spirit amongst young squash athletes by supporting them to participate in international events and eventually produce world squash champions from India.

Venue : D.Y. Patil Sports Academy, Nerul

Combat Sports

JUDO

The Mumbai Judo Association regulates the activity of Judo in the State and is a member of the Judo Federation of India. The member district bodies hold tournaments as per the rules of the Judo Federation of India for their districts and send the winners to the State Tournaments. As a result the State Tournaments held by the MJA yield the best teams for Mumbai and send them to the National Tournament. By a similar process the interstate tournaments at the national level held by the JFI results in producing the team of the best players from India who participate in the international events.

Tournament Dates: 9th to 11th December 2016

Venue : D.Y. Patil Sports Academy, Nerul

Last day for student registration: 30th September 2016

TAEKWONDO

The Taekwondo Association of Maharashtra is known for its efforts towards the development and progress of the combat sport of Taekwondo in the state of Maharashtra. The association has worked tirelessly to produce athletes who have showcased outstanding talent at State as well as National level. Taekwondo Association of Maharashtra hopes to become a strong platform for children and youth to learn Taekwondo and thus achieve higher accolades in the sport. The sport of Taekwondo at SFA Mumbai 2016 will be managed by Eklavya Taekwondo Academy established in the year 1992 by Usha Shirke, who has more than 23 years of experience in physical fitness and sports for children.

Tournament Dates: 17th and 18th December 2016

Venue : D.Y. Patil Sports Academy, Nerul

Last day for student registration: 30th September 2016
