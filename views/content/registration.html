Handball

Having a strong presence at the grass roots level in the city, Mumbai Handball Academy has made significant efforts to take the Olympic sport of Handball to the next level. While abundant talent exists at the inter-school level, Handball requires the right platform for the young athletes to showcase their talent at national and international sporting events. The Mumbai Handball Academy hopes to bridge the gap in the years to come.
