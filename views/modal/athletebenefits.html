BENEFITS FOR A REGISTERED SFA ATHLETE

Overall benefits that an athlete can avail by being a registered member athlete of SFA:

SFA Championship SFA MUMBAI 2017

Sporting experience at par with International sporting standards

  • State-of-the-art,multi-sporting infrastructure & facilities
  • HD Match Videography capturing every game of an athlete
  • High Quality Competition – A chance to compete with the Mumbai’s best sporting talent
  • Secure & Hygienic sporting environment
  • Immediate First Aid Care with Physiotherapists & Physicians stationed at all sporting arenas
  • All sports conducted as per Standard, Fair practices by Certified Match Officials
  • Access to Hydration & Nutrition centre’s with Complimentary Energy Drinks for each athlete
  • Scouting of talented athletes across different sports with an opportunity to win training & development scholarship along with complimentary mental & fitness assessment programs
  • Athletes can avail training in the sport of their choice with SFA’s Training & Development Programs*
  • Eligible to win Special Hampers & Cash Prizes in the following award categories:
    • Most promising male athlete
    • Most promising female athlete
    • Athlete participating in the maximum number of sports
  • No entry fees applicable to parents or other visitors accompanying an athlete from a registered school.
Advanced SPORTS SESSIONS

Complimentary Sport sessions by leading professionals for all athletes of the school

  • Fitness boot Camp
  • Hydration Clinic
  • Nutrition Workshop
  • Sports Workshops & Fitness Clinics for athletes
  • Special Sport tutorials for athletes & non-athletes (Shooting, Archery etc)
  • Special Days (Opening Ceremony, Girls Day, Closing Ceremony & other special days)
SCHOOL SPORTS ONLINE

Comprehensive Online School Sporting Profiles accessible on the SFA Portal (WWW.SFANOW.IN)

  • Athlete sporting profiles comprising of:
    • HD Match Videos of all the games played across each sport & event category
    • Athlete’s SFA Track Record
    • Year on year Match Statistics
    • Achievements across every sport & event category
    • Opportunity to get match video analyzed by experts in selected sports
  • Photo & Video Gallery capturing all the sporting action
  • Fixtures, Draws & Match schedules to be published online prior to the championship

*All Training & Development Programs to be announced one month prior to the championship

*All special awards to be announced one month prior to the championship
