BENEFITS FOR A SCHOOL REGISTERED WITH SFA

Overall benefits that a school and its students (Athletes & Non Athletes) can avail by being a registered member school of SFA:

SFA Championship SFA MUMBAI 2017

Be a part of the largest professional youth sporting championship!

  • Schools are eligible to participate across 25+ Sports
  • Eligible to be featured on the School Ranking Table (Ranking of Schools as per the sporting performance)
  • Automatic Qualification for special awards. Eligible to win Special Hampers & Cash Prizes in the following award categories:
    • School participating in the maximum number of sports
    • School with maximum win%
    • Best coaching team of a school
      *All special awards to be announced once registrations are closed
  • Discounted athlete registration fees for all participants from registered school
  • No entry fee is applicable to parents or any other visitors accompanying athletes of registered schools.
Advanced SPORTS SESSIONS

Complimentary Sport sessions by leading professionals for all the students of the school (Athletes & Non-Athletes)

  • Fitness boot Camp
  • Hydration Clinic
  • Nutrition Workshop
  • Sports Workshops & Fitness Clinics for athletes
  • Special Sport tutorials for athletes & non-athletes (Shooting, Archery etc)
  • Special Days (Opening Ceremony, Girls Day, Closing Ceremony & other special days)
